OUR STORY

From the fishing villages of Galachipa to the forefront of experimental fashion

"HEEMS isn't just clothing—it's a revolution stitched with the threads of our Bangladeshi heritage and the boundless creativity of our people."

— Yasir Himel, Founder

THE JOURNEY

How HEEMS came to be

1

2005: Humble Beginnings

At just 14 years old, Yasir Himel began creating clothing from scrap fabrics in his small Galachipa home. Using his mother's sewing machine and discarded fishing nets as inspiration, he crafted his first experimental designs.

2

2012: The First Breakthrough

After years of honing his craft, Yasir's "Monsoon Collection" caught the attention of a visiting designer from Dhaka. This led to his first professional collaboration and the realization that his unique perspective had value in the fashion world.

3

2018: HEEMS is Born

With savings from his collaborations, Yasir officially founded HEEMS in Galachipa. The name combines "Himel" with "Dreams," representing his vision to empower local artisans while creating boundary-pushing fashion.

4

2020: International Recognition

HEEMS' "Delta Blues" collection went viral during Dhaka Fashion Week, earning features in Vogue India and Dezeen. The collection's innovative use of traditional jamdani weaving in contemporary silhouettes put Bangladeshi experimental fashion on the global map.

5

Today: The Future

HEEMS now employs over 50 local artisans in Galachipa while collaborating with designers worldwide. Yasir's vision remains unchanged: to create fashion that honors Bangladeshi heritage while fearlessly innovating for the future.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

What makes HEEMS different

01

Rooted in Bangladesh

Every HEEMS piece begins with traditional Bangladeshi textiles and techniques—from jamdani weaving to nakshi kantha embroidery—reimagined through a contemporary lens.

02

Radical Innovation

We combine age-old craftsmanship with cutting-edge techniques like 3D textile printing and zero-waste patterning to create truly unique garments.

03

Community First

HEEMS isn't just a brand—it's a movement. We provide fair wages, skills training, and creative ownership to our artisans, proving that ethical fashion can be avant-garde.

MEET YASIR HIMEL

Born in 1991 in Galachipa, a small coastal town in southern Bangladesh, Yasir Himel grew up surrounded by the vibrant colors of fishing boats and the rhythmic patterns of woven nets. These childhood impressions would later become the foundation of his design philosophy.

Despite having no formal fashion education, Yasir's innate creativity and deep connection to his cultural roots enabled him to develop a distinctive aesthetic that blends traditional Bangladeshi craftsmanship with bold, contemporary silhouettes.

Today, Yasir splits his time between Galachipa—where he oversees the HEEMS workshop—and Dhaka, where he collaborates with emerging Bangladeshi designers to push the boundaries of South Asian fashion.
