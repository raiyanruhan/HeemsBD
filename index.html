From Village to Visionary

1995

Humble Beginnings

Started with a small tailoring shop in Galachipa, crafting traditional garments for local community.

2005

First Recognition

Won regional craftsmanship award for innovative fusion of traditional and contemporary designs.

2012

Dhaka Expansion

Opened first boutique in Dhaka, introducing the brand to urban fashion enthusiasts.

2018

International Debut

Showcased collection at London Fashion Week, receiving critical acclaim for sustainable practices.

2023

Global Recognition

Featured in Vogue as one of the most influential sustainable fashion brands from South Asia.
