About Human Dimension

We are a non-profit organization working at the intersection of human development and environmental conservation. Our mission is to create sustainable solutions that benefit both people and the planet.

Since our founding in 2010, we've partnered with communities in over 20 countries to implement programs that promote biodiversity, sustainable livelihoods, and cultural preservation.

"A generous person will prosper; whoever refreshes others will be refreshed. Our work is rooted in the belief that human wellbeing is intrinsically connected to the health of our natural world."
